La Chatelaine is a family owned French bakery and resturant with three locations in Columbus, OH. We serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner along with delicious pastries for desert. We invite you to relax in our warm atmosphere for any meal of the day. We will be glad to see you there. Learn more about our story on the "About" tab.

Champagne tasting Laurent Perrier

Nov 16, 2018

Laurent-Perrier has been crafting traditional and elegant Champagnes for over two centuries. Nestled in a grand cru village, the house is family-run, fiercely independent, and focused exclusively on producing Champagnes of unparalleled freshness, elegance and finesse. Jessica Starry, CSW, Regional Manager for Champagne Laurent-Perrier US, will share the story of the House of Laurent-Perrier and lead us through a unique tasting experience featuring Laurent-Perrier's innovative and comprehensive range of highly rated and sought-after Champagnes. $35pp 614-488-1911 reservations 1550 west lane avenue location
